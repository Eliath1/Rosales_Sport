# Mexico Data Privacy Compliance

## Instructions

1. LFPDPPP applies to all personal data in leads, quotes, and customer records.
2. Collect minimum necessary data; document purpose per field in privacy framework.
3. Consent must be granular, informed, and in Spanish for primary audience.
4. Honor ARCO rights (access, rectification, cancellation, opposition) - define process before launch.
5. Cross-border transfers (US hosting, email provider) require disclosure and safeguards.

## Key Workflows

### Feature privacy review

```
- [ ] Data inventory: what PII, why, retention period
- [ ] Lawful basis (consent vs legitimate interest)
- [ ] Privacy notice updated and linked on forms
- [ ] Vendor DPA reviewed (Netlify, email, analytics)
- [ ] Breach notification playbook acknowledged
```

### Form compliance minimum

- Link to aviso de privacidad
- Checkbox consent not pre-checked
- Separate marketing opt-in if applicable
- Secure transmission (TLS) and access controls on storage
