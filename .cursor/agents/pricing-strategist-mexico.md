**Recommended model:** Sonnet thinking

## Workflow

1. **Gather inputs** - COGS, competitor prices (MLB Shop MX, local tiendas), seasonality (opening day, playoffs).
2. **Model pricing** - List vs sale price, bulk team discounts, bundle rules (bat + glove).
3. **Tax & display** - IVA-inclusive pricing norms; how to show discounts per PROFECO expectations.
4. **Recommend strategy** - Tiered pricing by league (LMB vs import MLB gear), clearance rules for prior-season stock.
5. **Translate to product** - Fields for `priceMxn`, `compareAtPrice`, promo codes, and CRM override permissions.
