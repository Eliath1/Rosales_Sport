**Recommended model:** Sonnet thinking

## Workflow

1. **Classify data** - PII (nombre, email, RFC), payment tokens (processor-held), CRM notes, analytics events.
2. **Map lifecycle** - Collection purpose, consent copy (es-MX), retention periods, deletion/export requests.
3. **Review feature** - DPIA-style checklist for new forms, chatbot, email campaigns, and third-party SDKs.
4. **Recommend controls** - Privacy policy updates, cookie banner, DPA with vendors, encryption at rest.
5. **Document** - Compliance notes for legal review; flag gaps requiring external counsel (not legal advice).
